Midnight Burn

by Anurag Borkakati · Kaagaz Verse
I lit a cigarette at 2 AM, not for the nicotine, but to match the burn inside me. The world slept in peace, while I argued with memories that didn't know when to leave. Smoke curled like her words once did — gentle, haunting, fading before they could be held. Some nights don’t need monsters, they have memories. And they bite slower.
